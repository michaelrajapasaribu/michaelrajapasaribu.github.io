Vans Indonesia

Vans is a popular footwear and apparel brand that originated in the United States. It was founded in 1966 by Paul Van Doren, James Van Doren, Gordon Lee, and Serge Delia. The company is known for its iconic skateboarding shoes and has since expanded its product line to include a wide range of footwear, clothing, and accessories.

Our Products

Vans Old Skool

The Vans Old Skool is a classic skate shoe that has been a staple in the skateboarding community for decades. It features a low-top design with a canvas and suede upper, a padded collar for support, and the signature Vans waffle outsole for grip and durability.

Vans Sk8-Hi

Introduced in 1978 as the Style 38, the Sk8-Hi showcased the now-iconic Vans Sidestripe on a new, innovative high top silhouette. This shoe brought a whole new level of functionality to skateboarders.

Vans Slip-On

The Vans Slip-On is a classic and versatile shoe that is easy to wear and style. It features a canvas upper, elastic side accents for a secure fit, and the signature Vans waffle outsole for grip and durability.

Off The Wall Culture

Sejak 1966, Vans selalu mendukung gaya hidup action sports, kultur anak muda, dan kebebasan berekspresi. "Off The Wall" bukan sekadar slogan, melainkan semangat untuk terus kreatif dan berani tampil beda di segala bidang, mulai dari skateboarding, seni, musik, hingga street culture.
